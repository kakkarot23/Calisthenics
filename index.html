How Progression Works

1 Build Your Foundation

Start with Level 1 (Absolute Beginner). Focus on basic joint conditioning, grip, core stability (Hollow Holds), and basic squats and push-ups.

2 Focus on Key Prerequisite Skills

Advanced skills (like the Planche or Front Lever) require massive straight-arm strength. Complete easier versions first before attempting higher levels.

3 Train Consistently

Toggle the "Completed" checkmark on any skill card or use the active stopwatch and countdown timers to measure hold-times. Consistency logs keep your streak alive!
